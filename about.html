Happy Frenchies Make The Best Frenchies!

The health and well-being of our dogs is our number one priority.

We are home-based breeders of beautiful, happy, and healthy French Bulldogs. We aim to breed good-natured French Bulldogs with great structure and type. Our Frenchie pups come in a variety of colors: blue, chocolate, lilac, and soon with a longer coat (fluffy). All of our puppies come with a health guarantee -- if a puppy develops a serious, congential illness in the first year life then we will replace it. We are located in Dallas, TX, and we hand-deliver our puppies across the U.S. La Bear Frenchies are living in over 20 states and counting. Our puppies are raised in a loving family environment (right in our living room), with tons of interaction and socialization from day 1 so they are ‘home ready’ for their forever families. We are located on acreage just south of Dallas, where all our Frenchies have ample room to run around and play.
